## Serienkonsistenz

Die Zillertal-Herzen-Cover folgen einem einheitlichen Stil:
- **Buch 1:** Goldene Lärchen, Herbst, warmes Licht

**Stil:** Painterly/impressionistisch, keine Gesichter, Setting-fokussiert
**Format:** 900x1440 (1:1.6 KDP-Ratio)
**Textfreiraum:** Oben für Titel, unten für Autorin

## Variante 1: Der Kirschbaum (Empfohlen)

```
Painterly illustration book cover, vertical format, professional publishing quality,
soft impressionistic style with warm edges.

A blooming cherry tree in full spring glory, standing beside a traditional Tyrolean
guesthouse in the early morning light. The tree is covered in delicate pink and white
blossoms, some petals gently falling to the ground. The guesthouse has warm amber
light glowing from its windows, with green shutters and flower boxes.

In the foreground, a wooden bench sits beneath the cherry tree — empty, waiting.
A single red wool shawl is draped over the bench, suggesting someone was just there.

The mountains in the background are soft blue with patches of remaining snow on
the highest peaks. The sky is a gentle gradient from soft pink at the horizon to
pale blue above.

The overall mood is hopeful, romantic, like the beginning of something new after
a long winter. Spring morning light, dew on the grass, birds implied but not shown.

Color palette: Soft pink and white cherry blossoms, warm golden morning light,
fresh green grass, pale blue mountains, touches of red from the shawl.

Impressionistic, dreamy quality with visible brushstrokes.
Leave generous empty space at top for dark title text and bottom for author name in cream.
```

**Symbolik:** Kirschbaum = Franz' Vermächtnis + Neuanfang; leere Bank = Warten; Schal = Rosina

## Variante 2: Der Gasthof im Frühling

```
Painterly illustration book cover, vertical format, professional publishing quality,
soft impressionistic style with warm edges.

A cozy alpine guesthouse in late spring, viewed from the garden. The building is
traditional Tyrolean architecture with wooden balconies, green shutters, and
window boxes filled with red geraniums.

The garden is in full bloom — cherry trees, apple trees, wildflowers. A stone path
leads to the front door, which stands slightly open, inviting.

In the middle ground, two garden chairs face each other across a small table with
a coffee pot and two cups. Nobody is sitting there, but the scene suggests recent
presence — or anticipation.

Mountains rise in the background, their peaks still white with snow while the
valleys are green and alive. The sky is soft blue with a few white clouds.

The overall mood is warm, inviting, like coming home after being away too long.
Late morning light creates soft shadows and highlights the textures of wood and stone.

Color palette: Warm earth tones, fresh greens, pink and white blossoms,
touches of red geraniums, soft blue sky.

Impressionistic style with visible brushstrokes.
Leave generous empty space at top for dark title text and bottom for author name.
```

**Symbolik:** Offene Tür = Einladung; zwei Stühle = Zweisamkeit; Kaffee = Rosinas Morgen

## Variante 3: Kirschblüten-Nahaufnahme

```
Painterly illustration book cover, vertical format, professional publishing quality,
soft impressionistic style with dreamy edges.

Close-up view of cherry blossom branches in full bloom, filling most of the frame.
The blossoms are delicate pink and white, with soft petals and tiny stamens.
Some petals are falling, caught mid-air.

Through the branches, we glimpse a Tyrolean village in the valley below —
small white houses with dark roofs, a church spire, mountains beyond. The village
is soft and out of focus, like a memory or a dream.

The light is early morning, golden and soft, filtering through the blossoms
and creating a warm glow. Some branches have small green leaves just beginning
to unfold.

The overall mood is intimate, hopeful, like seeing the world through new eyes.
The cherry blossoms are the star, but the village below grounds the image in place.

Color palette: Soft pink blossoms, pale white petals, golden morning light,
soft green leaves, misty blue-gray village below.

Very impressionistic, almost watercolor-like quality.
Leave generous empty space at top for title and bottom for author name.
```

**Symbolik:** Nahaufnahme = Intimität; fallende Blüten = vergängliche Schönheit; Dorf darunter = Heimat
